SEAPORT'88 GATHERING

Ada bas… ada lori… Ayam memang best masak ngan kari… Once again we’re gonna have a party… To celebrate Class of ’88 35th Anniversary.

After almost 3 years since the pandemic hit us and with the MCOs…etc…, we are now moving towards and in the stage of endemic, it’s just couldn’t be a better time to celebrate our 35th Anniversary!

For those who have missed out, kali ni, it’s our 5th edition of Anniversary Celebration. Quick recap:

    1998 – 10th Anniversary
    2009 – 20th Anniversary
    2013 - 25th Anniversary
    2018 – 30th Annversary

" A Sweet Friendship Refreshes The Soul"

In 2018, we had the largest turnout of 128 including 5 teachers! Well done and thank you to all Seaportians who have directly and indirectly contributed in growing the attendance list back then.

We hoping for a similar number of attendees. So far in our database we have 221 contacts for the whole of Class of ’88. Kalo semuo boleh datang, this 35th Anniversary will go down in Hari Ini Dalam Sejarah.

Nonetheless, if you can take some time off and join us to catch up, that’ll be awesome!. So, long story short, here are the details:
